Association of Women in EECS

My roles:

  • Industrial Relations [communicate with tech companies to gain sponsorship and form partnerships for Breaktimes and info sessions]
  • Treasurer [manage budgets, perform reimbursements, move our financial management from inefficient practices to commercial banking] (Spring 2015)

About AWE:

AWE is an organization at UC Berkeley that focuses on empowering aspiring UC Berkeley women pursuing Computer Science or Electrical Engineering disciplines. Our main events are weekly lunches called "Breaktimes" with female engineers from companies like Dropbox, Oracle, Palo Alto Networks, and more. We also hold info sessions and intersocietal events with organizations like HKN, IEEE, and SWE.

My role:

  • House Leader [organize socials for a subset of SWE members who are in my "house"]

About SWE:

SWE is an organization that stimulates women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders, expands the image of the engineering profession as a positive force in improving the quality of life, and demonstrates the value of diversity.
